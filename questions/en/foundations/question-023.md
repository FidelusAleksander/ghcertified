> GitHub Copilot Free is available to all personal GitHub accounts, providing limited code completions and chat interactions per month. Paid plans such as Copilot Pro, Business, and Enterprise offer higher limits and additional features. Students, teachers, and maintainers of popular open source projects can get Copilot Pro for free.
