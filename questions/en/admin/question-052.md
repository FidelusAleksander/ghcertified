---
question: "How can an organization owner change the role of a member within the organization?"
documentation: "https://docs.github.com/en/organizations/managing-peoples-access-to-your-organization-with-roles/roles-in-an-organization"
---

- [x] By navigating to the `People` tab in the Organization and adjusting the member's role from the list.
- [ ] Members can change their own roles within the organization settings.
- [ ] By deleting and re-inviting the member with the desired role.
- [ ] Role changes within an organization are not possible; a new organization must be created.
