---
question: "What are the default permissions of a member in a GitHub organization?"
documentation: "https://docs.github.com/en/organizations/managing-peoples-access-to-your-organization-with-roles/roles-in-an-organization"
---

- [x] Members can create private repositories (subject to the organization's policy) and participate in projects within the organization to which they are granted access.
- [ ] They have the ability to delete the organization and transfer all ownership to another user without any approval or restriction from other owners.
- [ ] They can manage billing information, update payment methods, view invoices, and modify the subscription plan for the entire organization.
- [ ] Members cannot interact with any of the organization's repositories or projects until they are promoted to the owner role by an admin.
