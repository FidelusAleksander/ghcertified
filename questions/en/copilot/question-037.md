---
question: "Which GitHub Copilot subscription plans support organization-level custom instructions?"
documentation: "https://docs.github.com/en/copilot/customizing-copilot/adding-organization-custom-instructions-for-github-copilot"
---

- [ ] All plans that include GitHub Copilot
- [ ] Copilot Pro+ and above
- [x] Copilot Business and Copilot Enterprise
> Organization custom instructions are configured in organization settings and require a Copilot Business or Enterprise plan. Repository-level custom instructions are available on all plans.
- [ ] Copilot Enterprise only
